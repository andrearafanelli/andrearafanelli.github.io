Hi :)

I am a graduated student in Statistics and Data Analysis and currently a PhD student in Artificial Intelligence for Society at
University of Pisa.

During my years as a student, I had the opportunity to get fascinated with the world of data and research. My research as a PhD student focuses on human-machine interaction, with a special emphasis on the explainability, trustworthiness, and ethics of AI systems. The focal point of my work is the Neuro-Symbolic aspect of Artificial Intelligence. As a result, I'm interested in combining Deep Learning models with logic-symbolic knowledge in my studies. My greatest objective is to be able to apply my findings to societal issues (AI4GOOD), especially in the fields of education (AI4EDUCATION) and health (AI4HEALTH).
