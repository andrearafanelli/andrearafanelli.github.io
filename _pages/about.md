I am a postdoc researcher at **[Amsterdam UMC](https://www.amc.nl/web/home.htm)** in the **[Translational AI Laboratory](https://trailab.nl/)** and in the **[Amsterdam Center for Computational Cardiology](https://computational-cardiology.nl/)**.

My work centres on the development of _Digital Biomarkers_ and AI-driven medical analytics. Particularly, I focus on applying AI and machine-learning techniques to biomedical data, especially in cardiology and translational medicine.

I hold a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence in February 2025 from the **[University of Pisa](https://phd-ai-society.di.unipi.it/students/andrea-rafanelli/)**.
